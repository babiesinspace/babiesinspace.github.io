Over a decade in the Service Industry taught me flexibility, communication, and that I enjoy working hard under pressure. Working in Enterprise SaaS tempered my long-term ability to manage my time effectively; and showed me a more empathetic, and patient approach to my professional relationships.

Both taught me how to laugh at myself.
